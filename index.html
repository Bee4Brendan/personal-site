About Me

// • INFJ • Sagitarius • OKC

If you didn't know already, I'm Brendan and I'm from Oklahoma. Currently, I'm embracing the possibility of mixing art and science by "weaving art into the web" as a Frontend Developer.

My strengths lie in my visual-spatial and problem solving abilities. They allow me to visualize different solutions to a problem and to create intuitive, user-friendly interfaces. I know what looks good, while taking the user's experience into account. From the logic to the aesthetics; from the front-end to the back-end; from the UI to the database; I have experience in all areas of software development. Frontend is where I thrive and where I want to focus my career.

I chose computer science initially for my love of video games (Halo + Destiny) but wanted to stay in Oklahoma, where there aren't many game developer opportunities. As a photographer, I developed my visual artistic abilities that translate into my web development work. You can view my work below or by navigating to my Instagram.

My other interests include watching the Oklahoma City Thunder, skateboarding, and working out. I also enjoy spending time outdoors in nature, exploring new places. Much of my photography is centered around animals, plants, and landscapes.

I am a dark mode addict and recovering perfectionist... light mode is not allowed here
